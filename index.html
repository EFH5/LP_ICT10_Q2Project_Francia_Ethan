About Us

Colegio De Las Pinas is a modern, professional educational institute that seeks to give the healthy minds of tomorrow a space to bloom. For over one day, Colegio De Las Pinas has given its all in ensuring that the next generation grows up to be the best they can be, with our long spanning tradition and values.

Our faciltiies are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, professional staff, and a focused curriculum that caters to the needs of every child, giving them the space and place needed to learn, grow, and thrive the best they can.
